# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent while giving clear feedback
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and documentation rather than people
- Help keep discussions welcoming and useful

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Personal attacks or abusive language
- Publishing private information without permission
- Sustained disruptive behavior in issues, pull requests, or discussions

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior, contact: support@automateintelligence.ai
